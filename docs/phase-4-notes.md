## Deposit Flow

User enters amount
↓
Create Deposit Record
↓
Initiate Fapshi Payment
↓
Receive Payment Link
↓
Redirect User
↓
Payment Completed
↓
Webhook Received
↓
Verify Payment
↓
Credit Wallet
↓
Create Ledger Entry
